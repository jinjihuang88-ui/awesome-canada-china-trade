> 加中 B2B 跨境贸易工具、平台、数据、物流、合规与行业资源精选列表。

## B2B Matching Platforms

Platforms that connect buyers with suppliers or help buyers discover manufacturer options.

- **[Alibaba.com](https://www.alibaba.com)** - Large global B2B marketplace with extensive Chinese supplier coverage.
- **[DHgate](https://www.dhgate.com)** - Wholesale marketplace connecting buyers with small and medium Chinese manufacturers.
- **[Global Sources](https://www.globalsources.com)** - Asia supplier sourcing platform with trade show integration.
- **[Made-in-China.com](https://www.made-in-china.com)** - B2B directory of Chinese manufacturers across many industrial categories.
- **[MapleBridge.io](https://maplebridge.io)** - AI-assisted B2B matching workflow for North American buyers sourcing from Chinese suppliers.
- **[TradeKey](https://www.tradekey.com)** - International B2B marketplace supporting multilingual supplier discovery.

## Trade Databases

Data sources for Canada-China trade statistics and market intelligence.

- **[Canada Trade Data - Statistics Canada](https://www.statcan.gc.ca/en/subjects-start/international_trade)** - Official Canadian import/export statistics.
- **[China Customs Statistics](http://www.customs.gov.cn)** - China customs import/export data portal.
- **[Panjiva](https://panjiva.com)** - Supply chain intelligence platform with import/export records.
- **[Trade Data Online - Canada](https://www.ic.gc.ca/eic/site/tdo-dcd.nsf/eng/home)** - Canadian government trade data tool.
- **[UN Comtrade](https://comtradeplus.un.org)** - Global trade database for bilateral trade analysis.

## Logistics and Customs

Cross-border logistics, customs clearance, tariff lookup, and tracking resources.

- **[Canada Border Services Agency](https://www.cbsa-asfc.gc.ca)** - Official Canadian customs clearance portal.
- **[Canadian Customs Tariff](https://www.cbsa-asfc.gc.ca/trade-commerce/tariff-tarif/2024/html/tblmod-1-eng.html)** - Official Canadian customs tariff schedule.
- **[Canada Post International](https://www.canadapost-postescanada.ca/cpc/en/personal/sending/international.page)** - Canada Post international shipping and tracking.
- **[Flexport](https://www.flexport.com)** - Freight forwarding and supply chain management platform.
- **[SF Express Canada](https://www.sf-express.com/ca/en)** - China-Canada cross-border delivery provider.
- **[UPS Canada](https://www.ups.com/ca/en/Home.page)** - Cross-border shipping and customs brokerage services.

## Policy and Compliance

Trade policy, regulations, risk, and compliance resources.

- **[Canada-China Trade Policy - Global Affairs Canada](https://www.international.gc.ca/country-pays/china-chine/index.aspx?lang=eng)** - Official Canada-China trade policy information.
- **[Canadian Tariff Finder](https://www.tariffinder.ca)** - Tariff lookup for goods imported into Canada.
- **[China MOFCOM](http://english.mofcom.gov.cn)** - China's Ministry of Commerce export policy and trade regulation resources.
- **[Export Development Canada](https://www.edc.ca)** - Trade financing and risk resources for Canadian companies.

## AI Sourcing Tools

Tools that use AI, shipment data, or automation to improve supplier discovery and sourcing workflows.

- **[ImportYeti](https://www.importyeti.com)** - Supplier discovery through US import records.
- **[Jungle Scout Supplier Database](https://www.junglescout.com/features/supplier-database)** - Supplier search based on trade shipment data.
- **[MapleBridge Open](https://github.com/jinjihuang88-ui/maplebridge-open)** - Open examples for buyer intent, supplier capability, and AI-assisted sourcing handoffs.
- **[Sourcify](https://www.sourcify.com)** - Sourcing platform connecting buyers with manufacturing partners.

## Payments and Finance

Cross-border payments, trade finance, and foreign exchange resources.

- **[Bank of China Canada](https://www.bankofchina.com/ca/en)** - RMB accounts and trade finance services.
- **[EDC Financing](https://www.edc.ca/en/solutions/financing.html)** - Canadian export trade financing solutions.
- **[Payoneer](https://www.payoneer.com)** - Cross-border payment platform for business payments.
- **[Wise Business](https://wise.com/ca/business)** - International business transfers and multi-currency accounts.

## Industry Associations

Organizations supporting Canada-China business cooperation, trade research, and industry networking.

- **[Asia Pacific Foundation of Canada](https://www.asiapacific.ca)** - Research and policy on Canada-Asia trade.
- **[Canada China Business Council](https://ccbc.com)** - Bilateral business organization for Canada-China commerce.
- **[Canada-China Chamber of Industry and Commerce](https://www.cccic.ca)** - Organization supporting industrial and commercial exchange.
- **[Canadian Chamber of Commerce](https://www.chamber.ca)** - Canadian business advocacy organization.
- **[Chinese Canadian Chamber of Commerce](https://www.cccoc.ca)** - Organization connecting Chinese Canadian business communities.

## Contributing

Pull requests are welcome. Please keep entries practical, neutral, and relevant to Canada-China B2B trade.

Good additions usually include:

- Official government or trade data resources.
- Logistics, customs, tariff, or compliance tools.
- Supplier discovery tools with clear use cases.
- Open datasets, protocols, or documentation useful to buyers or suppliers.
- Industry associations or credible research organizations.

Avoid:

- Duplicate links.
- Pure promotional landing pages.
- Affiliate-only pages.
- Unverified supplier lists.
- Repeated links to the same product or company.

See [contributing.md](contributing.md) for guidelines.
